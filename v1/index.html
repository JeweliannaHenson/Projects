About me

My name is Sidney,

I have been practicing with photography since I was 12, I fell in love with the idea of capturing beauty in the world. I am 18 years old, and working in portrait photography. I love capturing people, their beautiful smiles, and their personalities. I love photography because it’s a chance to get to know people and to show my creativity.Getting to know people is so special to me because everyone is their own kind of unique. It’s a way to inspire and encourage others, It makes people feel beautiful and happy. Photography makes me happy, and my goal is to make others happy with my photography.
